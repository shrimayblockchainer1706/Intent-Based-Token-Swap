# 🤖 Intent-Based Token Swap using AI Agents

Welcome to the **Intent-Based Token Swap** project! This tool makes trading cryptocurrency as easy as sending a text message. 

## 📖 The Problem
Right now, swapping tokens in Decentralized Finance (DeFi) is confusing and technical. Users have to manually check different platforms for the best prices, worry about hidden fees, and deal with complicated interfaces. It’s too hard for the average person to just trade what they want.

## 🎯 Our Solution
We are building a system where you can just type what you want to do in plain English (your "intent"), like:  
👉 *"Convert 1 ETH to USDC"* An intelligent AI Agent takes that simple command and automatically does all the hard work: finding the best route, checking fees, and preparing the trade for you.

## ✨ Key Features
- **🗣️ Natural Language Input:** Just type what you want to trade in everyday language.
- **🗺️ Smart Routing:** The AI automatically scans multiple Decentralized Exchanges (DEXs) to find you the best possible price.
- **🛡️ Safe & Simulated:** It tests (simulates) the trade behind the scenes before you click approve, so you know exactly what you will get.
- **💰 Cost Optimized:** Designed to give you the best exchange rate, the lowest gas fees, and minimal slippage (price changes).
- **✅ You Are in Control:** The AI sets everything up, but it clearly explains the process to you. Nothing happens until *you* approve the final transaction.

## ⚙️ How It Works (The Rules)
- **EVM Compatible:** Works with popular blockchains like Ethereum, Polygon, Arbitrum, etc.
- **100% Non-Custodial:** We never hold your money. Your funds stay in your wallet, and you keep total control.
- **Fast & Accurate:** Operates in real-time so you always get the most up-to-date market prices.

## 🌟 What to Expect
A smooth, stress-free token swapping experience. You just tell the AI what you want, and it efficiently executes the trade with total transparency and zero headache.
